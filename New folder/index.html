1. Baba Anandeshwar Temple.

Most spiritual place to see lord shiv Mahadev in Kanpur near Ganga River, Everyone feels energetic fresh and calm at this temple.

2. Brahmavart Ghat

Being a religious place , very less known to people, it is peaceful, beautiful ghats of ganga, temple of religious significance.

3. Dwarkadhish Temple

Its a very beautiful holy place. The temple is dedicated t Lord Krishna.Its a very calm and holy place .

4. Green Park Stadium

Stadium is very nice i saw a match in this stadium india vs south africa the view of ground was good if you are sitting in balcony.

5. Harcourt Butler Technological Institute

This is under very big university which is number 1 university in Uttar Pradesh. Lots of greenery can be seen here.

6. ISKCON Kanpur

Fabulous and peaceful place to visit in kanpur it is the best place to visit in kanpur with family or for couples .

7. Jagannath Temple

This is heritage place which is very old as well full of art all around and natural things around this.
There was many devotees came on week ends day. Peaceful location. Temple was old but beautiful.

8. Jain Glass Temple

This is local sightseeing place, not a very big place but good to see at-least once t understand our culture.

9. Shri Radhakrishna Temple

One of the most beautiful temple in KANPUR. Excellent Architecture. Pleasant ambience. Well maintained and beautiful surroundings like garden, lighting, fountain and many more.. must visit at least once time in life. You feel peace their.

10. Kanpur Memorial Church

Kanpur Memorial Chruch is a holy place for Christians. It is open to anybody who wishes to visit and pray.

11. Valmiki Ashram Temple

This ashram is so good to see and architecture is like fort. Nice and very cleanliness. Very peaceful place it is.
